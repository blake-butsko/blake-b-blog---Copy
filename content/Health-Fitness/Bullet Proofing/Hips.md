*For any impingement in your joints you first want to
Test - to see what range(s) of motions are suffering and determine your approach to either 
Lengthen - a lack of mobility is causing issue
Strengthen - an imbalance is causing issue (either across the same joint like the hip or along the chain) + stability work*

##### Basic 80-20 Solver for Most Hip Issues
This is a basic 80/20 solver for most hip issues (especially for the squat) should be done every other day, before leg, days, or whatever frequency works best for you, very easy on the joints
