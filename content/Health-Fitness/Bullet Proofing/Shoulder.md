##### Rotator Cuff Strengthening

Test, Strengthen, Lengthen

Each exercise done **3 x 8-12**
In practice I could only do half of a first set, then 2nd and 3rd, it ends up being like 2 sets are just fine - weight should start at none and just work up slowly, you will get sore, of course use enough weight that you feel it working, nut if you have existing injury take additional caution until you build it back up and regain your safety
The way down is more important than the way up -  controlling of the weight and moving it well
