Rotator Cuff Strengthening

3-Step “Reverse Out Shoulder Pain” Routine
long form version, short summary is fine

Test, Strengthen, Lengthen

Each exercise done 3 x 8-12
In practice I could only do half of a first set, then 2nd and 3rd, it ends up being like 2 sets are just fine - weight should start at none and just work up slowly, you will get sore, of course use enough weight that you feel it working, nut if you have existing injury take additional caution until you build it back up and regain your safety
The way down is more important than the way up - controlling of the weight and moving it well


Rest your elbow right on your quad or elbow whatever is comfortable
Fight it down, dead stop at bottom, explode up (when you’re able to), that is our safety goal where we know we can control the load
no motion in the body other than rotation
Goal: 10% body weight, EX. 20 lbs at 200 bodyweight


Lower over 3-5 seconds
this is going to be hard, pay attention to how his knees are place to position yourself correctly
You’ll have to play with what angle the bench should be at
Slowly slowly load




Keep arms at 45, engage glutes but don’t arch back
Slight amount of momentum then hold for a 1/2 second at the top and fight the way down 2-3 seconds
I’ve always used an adjustable bench and just rested my forehead on it, ensure the bench is low enough that you’re not miss during that range of motion
Again just no weight is great, then slowly add
