For any impingement in your joints you first want to
Test - to see what range(s) of motions are suffering and determine your approach to either
Lengthen - a lack of mobility is causing issue
Strengthen - an imbalance is causing issue (either across the same joint like the hip or along the chain) + stability work

Knees Over Toes for Rebuilding and Getting back to a Baseline

all done with 3x12

  1. Scaled by bench angle (lower is harder I think) and dumbells

    example of it loaded

  2. Scaled by dumbbell

  3. Abs to bench, scaled by barbell load (can also do this with dumbbell through the legs) - Push out knees while doing this 5 reps 75% bodyweight

    Optional:

  4. Hip Flexor Cable pull 20 reps 50% body weight - “Driving Knees towards chest” - hip flexor health more time efficient to do it here than knee day - this will help put less pressure on the knees when running because you don’t have to slam them you can use that flexor
